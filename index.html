Questions You'll Want Answered

Most APC Solar customers reduce their monthly bill by 60–90%. A homeowner paying Rs. 50,000/month can realistically bring that down to Rs. 3,000–8,000/month. The exact savings depend on your consumption and the system we recommend for your home.
With a hybrid system, your home continues running on solar power seamlessly during outages. Basic grid-tied systems without batteries turn off during outages by NEPRA safety requirement. We'll recommend the right configuration for your needs during the consultation.
Most residential installations are completed in 1 to 3 days. We work efficiently and cleanly — our team respects your home. Once installed, your system begins generating energy immediately.
Yes — we assist with the complete net metering application and K-Electric approval process. Net metering lets you export excess solar energy back to the grid for bill credits. This can reduce your bill to near zero or credit you toward the following month.
Most homeowners in Karachi see a full payback in around 3.5 years, after which the system generates essentially free electricity for another 18+ years. Given continuously rising electricity prices, the payback period is shrinking every year.
We don't disappear after installation day. Our after-sales team is reachable by WhatsApp and phone (9 AM – 9 PM, 7 days a week). The main maintenance a solar system needs is regular panel cleaning to keep efficiency at its best. Dust accumulation on panels reduces output, so periodic cleaning — especially during dry months — makes a meaningful difference to your savings. Beyond that, the system runs automatically with no moving parts and no ongoing fuel costs.
